The Five Elements - Wood, Earth, Water, Fire, and Metal - are believed to be the fundamental elements of everything in the universe between which interactions occur.

WOOD

Weaken - Wood absorbs Water

Destroy - Wood roots Earth

EARTH

Weaken - Earth cannot burn by Fire

Destroy - Earth dams Water

WATER

Weaken - Water rusts Metal

Destroy - Water extinguishes Fire

FIRE

Weaken - Fire burns Wood

Destroy - Fire melts Metal

METAL

Weaken - Metal excavates Earth

Destroy - Metal cuts Wood

The Elements are all connected. Each element both controls and is controlled by another element. The system is connected, moving, and constantly in a process of balance.

Now go and choose your Element and see if you will weaken your opponent or if you will control and destroy!
